## Slide 7 — The judgment layer

## Things AI still can't do for you

- Decide **what** to build
- Notice when the code looks right but is wrong
- Choose between three correct-looking designs
- Hold the user in mind when the model can't
- Say no to scope creep

> **This is the judgment layer. It's teachable. It's the point of this curriculum.**

*[Visual: Bullets fade in one at a time. The pull-quote highlights in accent after the last bullet lands.]*
